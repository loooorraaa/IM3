CURRENSEA

SURF THE EXCHANGE

Just like the ocean, currency markets are always in motion, rising and falling in waves. With CurrenSea, you can track these movements, compare different currencies and spot trends at glance. We turn complex exchange rate data into clear, wave-shaped visualizations - helping you stay on the right wave.

Our platform monitors global exchange rates with the  Australian Dollar (AUD) as the reference point. All currency data is sourced directly from the Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA) through the Exchange Rates API.
